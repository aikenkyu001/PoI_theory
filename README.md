## 🧠 Overview

**Physics of Intelligence (PoI)** is a groundbreaking academic framework that redefines intelligence not as a computational process of information handling, but as a rigorous system of geometric dynamics occurring on physical manifolds.

### The Core Hierarchy
- **PKGF (Mathematical Infrastructure):** Provides the strictly linear, operator-theoretic backbone based on partial differential equations (PDE) and operator theory.
- **PoI (Physical Theory):** Extends PKGF by introducing non-linear, substrate-invariant physical processes such as phase transitions, rank jumps, and gauge breaking.

## 🛠 Theoretical Framework: The CDU Cycle

The fundamental essence of intelligent activity is formulated through the **CDU Cycle**—an irreversible three-phase physical process:

1.  **Construction (Cause):** Formation of logical structures by adapting to external Semantic Potential ($\Omega$).
2.  **Dissipation (Divergence):** Structural collapse and abstraction driven by the dissipative operator $\mathcal{D}(K)$, reducing redundant rank.
3.  **Unification (Metabolic Phase):** Emergence of new order and dimensional transitions through non-linear phase transitions.
